Polymarket Wallet Lens

Analyze one Polymarket wallet, or browse sports and crypto leaderboards of systematic favorite-buyers. Pure client-side; reads public Polymarket data.

Goal advantage validation

Historical football triggers where a team extends a one-goal lead into a two-goal lead. Filter by league and by the minute when the advantage became two goals.

Hold-to-expiry model: win = the held outcome settled at 1 (curPrice == 1), regardless of what the wallet actually did. PnL is hypothetical — as if every position were held to resolution: +shares×(1−entry) on a win, −stake on a loss. The wallet's own realized trading PnL is ignored. Only settled positions count, and all stats are scoped to entry price ≥ the floor (default 0.95). Sport/bet type inferred from slugs. Data: data-api.polymarket.com/closed-positions.
